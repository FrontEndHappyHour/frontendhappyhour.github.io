About

Front End Happy Hour is a software engineering podcast that started in February 2016. The podcast consists of a regular group of panelists from Silicon Valley companies, Netflix, Twitch, Atlassian, Evernote, and LinkedIn, who are often joined by guests who get together over drinks to discuss a variety of topics for frontend, JavaScript, software engineering, and career development.

The Front End Happy Hour podcast is recorded regularly and releases new episodes bi-weekly.
