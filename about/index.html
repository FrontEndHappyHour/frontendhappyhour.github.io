About

Front End Happy Hour is a software engineering podcast that first started in February 2016. The podcast consists of a regular group of panelists from Silicon Valley companies, Netflix, Twitch, Atlassian, Evernote, and LinkedIn who are often joined by guests that get together over drinks to discuss a variety of topics for frontend, JavaScript, software engineerning, and career development.
