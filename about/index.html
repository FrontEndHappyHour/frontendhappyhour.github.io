About

Front End Happy Hour is a podcast that first started in February 2016. The podcast consists of a regular group of panelists from Silicon Valley companies, Netflix, Twitch, Atlassian, Evernote, and LinkedIn who are often joined by guests that get together over drinks to discuss a variety of topics for frontend development, software development, and career development.

The Front End Happy Hour podcast is recorded on a regular basis and releases new episodes on a bi-weekly basis. The panelists record in-person around a table in one location to help create a casual conversation on each of the topics.
